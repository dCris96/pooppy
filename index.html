Our Mission

Poopy Love's mission is to merge the love of dogs with environmental responsibility and community living. We are dedicated to providing high-quality, environmentally friendly solutions for pet waste management, starting with our dog waste bags. Beyond selling bags, we seek to empower pet owners to be responsible stewards of their canine companions and shared environment. We believe in environmental responsibility through biodegradable bags, community advocacy, and education about the importance of keeping public spaces clean. We look for innovation and convenience to make waste collection easier. In addition, we are socially committed, contributing to improving the lives of homeless dogs and charities. At Poopy Love, we believe that love of dogs and responsibility go hand in hand, working to build a future where our canine companions and community thrive in a clean and healthy environment.
